Authentic East African Pilau

Pilau is a rich, aromatic rice dish enjoyed across East Africa. Packed with spices and flavor, it is perfect for family gatherings or celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g beef or goat meat, cubed
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 tbsp pilau masala
  • 3 cups beef broth or water
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot and fry onions until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and pilau masala; stir for 1 minute.
  3. Add meat and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Pour in broth and bring to a boil.
  6. Add rice, salt, and black pepper; mix gently.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with kachumbari.
